Welcome to the GEODistricts 2021 redistricting home page. GEODistricts is a public-facing web mapping tool for identifying districts and communities of interest.

Redistricting is the process of adjusting election district boundaries every 10 years after the release of the U.S. Census. This process applies to governing bodies with elected representatives, including federal, state, and local governments. The goal of redistricting is to create population-balanced districts for equal representation that reflect the demographic changes of the past 10 years.

This is an exciting opportunity to contribute your input to update election districts for the next 10 years. Click the button for your area to get started.
